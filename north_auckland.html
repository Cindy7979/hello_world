AUCKLAND

Auckland is New Zealand's largest city, home to over a third of the population. Be sure not to miss the many pleasures offered by the "City of Sails".

- DEVONPORT
Devonport is situated on a peninsula across from the CBD, making it home to numerous beaches with calm waves and views of Rangitoto Island. Mount Victoria provide a splendid 360 degree view of Auckland and the surrounding harbours. ( website :: http://www.visitdevonport.co.nz/ )

- WAIHEKE ISLAND
Waiheke Island is an excellent summer location with forests, beaches and winery tours.

- MATAMATA
Hobbiton is located here, a village from the popular Lord of the Rings trilogy.
